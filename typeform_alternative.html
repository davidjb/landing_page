What is the best alternative to TypeForm?

Typeform offers a unique design experience, but as a closed-source project it's hard to customize to your own specific needs. With TellForm, you get the awesome design found in TypeForm with customizability that can only be found in an opensource project. TellForm allows you to build beautiful forms and automate tasks across your entire company or organization.
